Stray Kids — Timeline

Aug 2017

Show announced

JYP announced the survival reality show Stray Kids.

Oct–Dec 2017

Reality show airs

The Mnet reality show aired (Oct 17 – Dec 19) featuring the trainees and debut process.

Oct 6, 2017

Pre-debut single: "Hellevator"

"Hellevator" released as a pre-debut digital single.

Jan 8, 2018

Pre-debut EP: Mixtape

Pre-debut EP Mixtape released — songs from the show.

Mar 25, 2018

Official debut — I Am Not

Debut showcase Unveil (Op. 01: I Am NOT) and EP I Am Not (title: "District 9").

2018

"I Am" series

Follow-up EPs: I Am WHO and I Am YOU.

Oct 2019

Lineup change

Member Woojin left the group (October 2019).

2019

Clé series

Released the Clé series: Clé 1: Miroh, Clé 2: Yellow Wood, Clé: Levanter.

2020

First studio album: Go Live

Released Go Live (first studio album) and made the Japanese debut with compilation SKZ2020.

2021

Noeasy — million seller

Released Noeasy, their first million-selling Korean studio album.

2022

Global push

Signed with Republic Records for U.S. promotions; released EPs Oddinary and Maxident.

2023

Albums & growth

Released Japanese album The Sound and Korean studio album 5-Star.

2024

Mixtape & EP

Released mixtape Hop and EP Ate.

2025

Studio album: Karma

Released studio album Karma.
