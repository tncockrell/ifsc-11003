Frequently Asked Questions

Why does Gojo wear a blindfold?

The Six Eyes allow him to see cursed energy in overwhelming detail, so he limits the input by covering his eyes. Removing the blindfold lets him perceive everything at maximum efficiency.

What is Limitless?

A powerful inherited Cursed Technique that lets Gojo manipulate space at an atomic level, creating various offensive and defensive effects.

What is the Infinity?

A defensive aspect of Limitless that slows and stops objects before they can reach him, effectively creating an infinite space around Gojo.

What are Gojo's other techniques?

Gojo's main techniques include Blue (attractive force), Red (repulsive force), and Hollow Purple (combination of Blue and Red for destructive power). Each has unique applications in combat.

What is the Six Eyes?

A rare hereditary ability that allows Gojo to see and analyze cursed energy with extraordinary precision, reducing energy consumption when using techniques.

Is Gojo really the strongest sorcerer?

Yes, he is widely recognized as the strongest jujutsu sorcerer alive, combining Limitless, Six Eyes, intelligence, and battle experience to overcome nearly any opponent.

What is Gojo’s role in Jujutsu Kaisen?

He is a mentor at Tokyo Jujutsu High, teaching and protecting students like Yuji Itadori, Megumi Fushiguro, and Nobara Kugisaki, while also maintaining balance in the jujutsu world.

Who are his closest allies?

Close allies include former classmates like Suguru Geto and Shoko Ieiri, as well as his students at Tokyo Jujutsu High. He collaborates with other powerful sorcerers to counter threats.

What is Gojo's personality like?

He is playful, confident, and often teases others, but he is also strategic, intelligent, and fiercely protective of his students. He strives to improve the jujutsu world even if it means challenging authority.

What are his most notable battles?

Key battles include fighting Toji Fushiguro during the Hidden Inventory Arc, participating in the Kyoto Goodwill Event, and confronting Kenjaku during the Shibuya Incident, showcasing his incredible mastery of techniques.

Can Gojo be defeated?

Although almost invincible, he can be temporarily neutralized using rare items like the Prison Realm. Even when sealed, his influence on the jujutsu world remains significant.
