Stray Kids is an 8-member K-pop group formed by JYP Entertainment. They officially debuted on March 25, 2018. Known for their high-energy perfomances and self-produced music, the members of Stray Kids write, compose and produce much of their own work - especially through the sub-unit 3RACHA (Bang Chan, Changbin, and Han).

Their music explores themes of youth, identity, rebellion, and empowerment. The fandom of Stray Kids is called STAY, reflecting the deep bond between the group and their audience. Each member also has a fun SKZOO animal character, a creative mascot that's part of their fandom identity.
